Simple Type ismcomplies:CVEnumISMCompliesWithValues
Namespace urn:us:gov:ic:cvenum:ism:complieswith
Annotations
(U) ISM rule sets documents may comply with. This CVE is used by @ism:compliesWith. PERMISSIBLE VALUES The permissible values for this simple type are defined in the Controlled Value Enumeration: CVEnumISMCompliesWith.xml
Diagram
Type restriction of xsd:token
Facets
enumeration USGov
Document claims compliance with all rules encoded in ISM for documents produced by the US Federal Government. This is the minimum set of rules for US documents to adhere to, and all US documents should claim compliance with USGov. For example, a US Intelligence Community document should claim ism:compliesWith="USGov USIC".
enumeration USIC
Document claims compliance with all rules encoded in ISM for documents produced by the US Intelligence Community. Documents that claim compliance with USIC MUST also claim compliance with USGov.
enumeration USDOD
Document claims compliance with all rules encoded in ISM for documents produced by the US Department of Defense. Documents that claim compliance with USDOD MUST also claim compliance with USGov.
enumeration OtherAuthority
Document claims compliance with an authority other than the USGov, USIC, or USDOD. This token is not allowed if the ism:ownerProducer contains USA.
